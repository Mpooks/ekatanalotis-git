About us

e-katanalotis SA. was founded in 2022 and is active in the development of innovative technology services, creating an ingenious e-commerce platform. Our business model relies on users' experience and their interaction with local stores, supermarkets and kiosks.


Based in Patras, e-katanalotis is believed to be the new leading digital brand behind the creation and development of the cutting-edge price, offer and product comparison engine our developers worked on. The website provides a user-centric platform, where users can interact with products by adding new offers and/or liking and disliking already existing ones. Finally, our rewarding system provides users with tokens, which can be earned through the like/dislike system at the end of each month. The tokens can be used by the earners to achieve additional and unique offers on various products.
